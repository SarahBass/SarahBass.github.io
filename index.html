Virtual Pets

Virtual Star Watch

For WearOS Watches, Virtual Pet Star changes transforms when you reach your step goal! Daily background changes that follow holiday and seasonal themes.

AR Star

Built in Unity, AR Star uses AR to show you a cute virtual pet. Earn Pets every day as you reach your exercise goals.

ABC Pup

A learning game that features collectible virtual dog pets. Travel space and learn to spell!

Virtual Pet Shiba

Virtual Pet Shiba, Pomeranian, and Dog are available in Fitbit Gallery. Feed your pet as you earn steps to change its mood. Monthly Changing backgrounds. 

Virtual Pixel Pet

Based on 90s style Virtual pets like Tamagotchis and Gigapets, you can level up your pet as you earn steps. Styles include Dinosaurs, Star Wars, Halloween, Retro, and more! Find in the Fitbit Gallery!

Virtual Pet Star

These Virtual Star Pets change what they do every hour, and transform into a surprise when you reach your goal. Daily changing backgrounds based on holidays and seasons.
